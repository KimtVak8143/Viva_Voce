Viva Voce Practice Bot

Description of Project :

This Bot is expected to answer to your Viva Questions for most CSE Subjects and Help you prepare for Viva Exams.

This Bot is powered by Azure Web Apps. And Several other Functionalities will be added in subsequent phases.

How to Use the tool:

Jump over to the subject you want to practice. Then select any specific topic or go All-in. Then Bot will ask you questions from respective topic and you can answer by typing out the answer or even by speech (this Functionality will be added later).

Or if you want to ask any Viva-centric questions to the bot, you can head over to the explore page and input your doubt statement, the bot will pull over the most appropriate answer from the database.
